About Us

Robeoutlet has been servicing their customers since 1997 and leading the market of wardrobe products in queensland. For all these years, we have been persistently working on developing our production: we use innovations, improve quality standards, and increase customer service levels. Today, Robeoutlet is a reliable producer and supplier whom hundreds of clients can trust; besides, we have increased the company assortment dozens of times.

To be closer to our dear customers, we have developed this site. Everyone who wants to order a wardrobe, can do this without leaving home. Convenient payment and delivery methods, chat with specialists, online calculators for cost computation  — we made everything to ease the purchase process and answer all the user requests.

In Brisbane and Gold Coast, we have our own delivery service. When you order, we guarantee maximum short delivery terms. We also cooperate with all the main logistics companies (TNT, AU post, Aramex, DHL), which deliver goods to your home or the nearest pickup warehouse.

Besides, we will be glad to meet you in our showroom, which is situated at the address: Shop 6/76 Bundall Rd, Bundall Bundall, QLD, Australia 4217. Visit us to see the articles closer and make sure of their high quality.

Superior quality at an affordable price

If you order goods with us, you will be sure you will buy high-quality and safe goods. They will be maximum comfortable to use and will not harm your health or the health of your loved ones.

We apply vinyl back on mirrors for safety purposes, which makes them maximum impact-resistant. In case of integrity damage, such a layer keeps the pieces and prevents their shattering around the room. All our products meet Australian standards AS/NZS 2208:1996 as determined by AS1288:2006 and have quality certificates which we provide at the customer’s request.

High-class specialists

For years of work, we have created a successful team where every employee values the company’s reputation! All our workers are highly experienced and love their job. Our support staff regularly undergoes training, visits production facilities, and knows everything about wardrobes.

To every customer, we guarantee qualitative consultation at all stages of the order, until they are fully satisfied!
